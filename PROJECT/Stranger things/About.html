About Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a popular science fiction and horror series created by the Duffer Brothers. The show is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana.

The story follows a group of young friends who face mysterious and supernatural events after the sudden disappearance of a boy. Their search uncovers secret government experiments and a dark parallel world known as the Upside Down.

The series is widely praised for its nostalgic 1980s references, engaging storytelling, and powerful performances. With its blend of horror, science fiction, and coming-of-age themes, Stranger Things has become a global cultural phenomenon and continues to influence modern entertainment.
