Dhundhar Region

Dhurandhar is a hit Indian spy-action duology written and directed by Aditya Dhar. The films follow an undercover Indian intelligence agent, Jaskirat Singh Rangi (played by Ranveer Singh), who infiltrates criminal syndicates in Karachi.

About Culture Places

Dhurandhar (2025):

Released on 5 December 2025, this first installment became a massive success, grossing over ₹1,300 crore worldwide. It is currently available for streaming on Netflix.

Dhurandhar: The Revenge (2026):

The sequel was released in theatres on 19 March 2026. It features an ensemble cast including Sanjay Dutt, R. Madhavan, and Arjun Rampal. The film has already crossed the ₹800 crore mark globally within its first week

Cast and Characters

  • Ranveer Singh - Jaskirat Singh Rangi / Hamza Ali Mazari
  • Sanjay Dutt - SP Chaudhary Aslam
  • Arjun Rampal - Major Iqbal
  • R. Madhavan - Ajay Sanyal
  • Akshaye Khanna - Rehman Dakait (Part 1 Antagonist)

Key Details :-

  1. Music : The high-energy soundtrack and background score are composed by Shashwat Sachdev.
  2. OTT Platform : While the first part is on Netflix, the digital rights for Dhurandhar: The Revenge have been acquired by JioHotstar.
  3. Reception: The series is praised for its technical brilliance and realistic action but has faced some criticism for its long runtimes—with the sequel clocking in at nearly 3 hours and 49 minutes.
